Volcano Judge Ash Subscription


Features
With the paid Ash subscription, users have the following advantages:

  • A unique Ash badge on your profile.
  • View private problems, and view non-contest problems during a contest.
  • Create a custom feature-rich bio with support for HTML, CSS, and Markdown.
  • Access C++ for general use, not just for contests. (Available with Ash+)
  • Access languages and features prior to official release. (Available with Ash+)
  • Create problems without review needed. (Available with Ash+)

Pricing
Here's the pricing for Ash:
  • Common Ash: $3.99/month
  • Ash+: $9.99/month
Every week, volcano runs a lottery (thanks volcano!). Email volcanojudge@gmail.com every Monday to enter the lottery. The winner receives two weeks of Common Ash for free. Common Ash is also available for winners in some contests and through some giveaways on Discord and the site.
