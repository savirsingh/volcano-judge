Volcano Judge - Project Shutdown

Check out my new project: Math Contest Repository

Date: January 25, 2024

Dear Volcano Judge Users,

I hope this message finds you well. I am writing to inform you about the shutdown of Volcano Judge, a project that I developed during my grade 10 year. It has been a rewarding experience working on this competitive programming platform, and I appreciate the support and engagement from the user community.

After over a year of working on Volcano Judge, I've decided it's time to move on and innovate to create more great things. Unfortunately, I am unable to devote my time to this project anymore and I don't believe in running projects which I'm too busy to maintain.

Volcano Judge is no longer available, but a similar platform I've created is the Math Contest Repository.

After careful consideration, I have decided to retire the project - at least temporarily. Managing Volcano Judge has been a valuable learning experience for me, and I have gained insights into the complexities of running such platforms. However, due to current commitments and challenges, I find it difficult to continue maintaining the project effectively - if you are able to properly manage Volcano Judge, please contact me so that I can transfer its ownership to new hands.

I want to express my gratitude to each one of you who has been a part of the Volcano Judge community. Your contributions, feedback, and participation have been instrumental in shaping the platform.

I understand that this news may be disappointing for some, and I sincerely apologize for any inconvenience caused. If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to me at savirsinghwork -at- gmail.com.

Thank you for your understanding and support.

Sincerely,
Savir
